Subaru Impreza

Home Page | View Larger Picture | Back

The Impreza is a compact hatchback manufactured by Japanese automaker Subaru. Although it may not look like much to the outsider, the vehicle features symmetrical all-wheel drive to allow comfortable driving in all conditions. In addition to its driving capabilities, the Impreza is equipped with Subaru's EyeSight safety system which includes Pre-Collision Braking, Pre-Collision Brake Assist, Pre-Collision Throttle Management, Adaptive Cruise Control, Lane Departure Warning, Lane Sway Warning, Lead Vehicle Start Alert and Lane Keep Assist.
